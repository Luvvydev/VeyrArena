Veyr Arena v61 objective/savepoint polish

Changed files:
- src/main.js
- styles.css

Fixes included:
- Retry HUD moved upward so it no longer blocks gun name or ammo area.
- Run failed/game over sounds reduced heavily.
- Tower checkpoint stabilization now costs Hope only, no supplies.
- Room progress is saved on room start and after room clear.
- Death retry button now retries the current saved floor and room instead of restarting from floor 1.
- Hold circle objectives spawn near the center of the map.
- Hold circle missions are slightly easier: lower hold time, slower reinforcement spawn, lower reinforcement cap, less direct objective rushing.
- Survive objectives are shorter and spawn reinforcements more slowly.
- Objective HUD is smaller and more transparent.
- NPC townfolk pause near the player so they are easier to talk to.
- Garden project visual is smaller and plot focused instead of a large upgrade building.
- Completed story chapters shrink into compact replay cards.
- Board job markers now show for all unfinished board jobs, not just the selected one.
- Pulse enemy echoes are hidden unless there is line of sight.
- Village next goal text updated to reflect Hope only checkpoint cost.
